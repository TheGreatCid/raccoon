ADCHSplitChemicalPotential

Chemical potential kernel in Split Cahn-Hilliard that solves chemical potential in a weak form

Uses automatic differentiation to solve for chemical potential in a weak sense (mu-mu_prop=0). Can be coupled to Cahn-Hilliard equation to solve species diffusion. Allows spatial derivative of chemical potential when coupled to material state such as stress, etc. Can be used to model species diffusion mediated creep. Typically used with ADCHSplitConcentration and can combined with ADCHSoretMobility to incorporate the effects of thermodiffusion.
